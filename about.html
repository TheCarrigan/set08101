About

Who are you?

You might be wondering if that's really me? Well, buckle up buttercup, because it is! I know, even I sometimes rub my eyes in disbelief that I'm the genuine article.

Never in my wildest dreams did I think my life would end up like this or that I'd have the time to conjour up a website. But here I am, soaking in all my fabulousness, while some guy dressed as Deadpool hogs the spotlight. I mean, come on, who does he think he is? Oh, wait...

Why did you start this website?

Why did I start this website, you ask? Well, honestly, it all began during a heated game of "Truth or Dare" with a herd of alpacas. I know, it sounds like a wild Friday night, right? So, when it was my turn, they dared me to create a website, and being the spontaneous, fun-loving person I am, I thought "Why the heck not?!"

What is your goal?

So, my grand plan was to create this ridiculously funny and entertaining website that would have people rolling on the floor laughing, resulting in a global shortage of tissues due to all the tears of joy. But, as it turns out, making a website is a lot more complicated than just stringing together cat memes and dad jokes. Now, my goal is to create a site that won't crash every five minutes, and might even teach people a thing or two.
